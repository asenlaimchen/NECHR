About NECHR

The Northeast International Centre for High School Research (NECHR) was founded with the primary objective of developing research at the academic level and critical thinking among students in the Northeast region of India. The NECHR, in its initial phases, intended to meet the educational imbalance experienced by the students in the backward region by offering resources, guidance, and opportunities to conduct high-level academic research. The center focused on equipping students with the abilities they would need to succeed in scientific inquiry, academic writing, and problem-solving, with a focus on the particular challenges and cultural contexts of the Northeast.

Through the years, NECHR expanded its horizon to provide access to high school students all over the world and expanded its objective towards making academic opportunities for research accessible globally. With the perception that education deficits span the entire globe, NECHR established its objective towards establishing a platform wherein students from everywhere in the globe would acquire educational and research opportunities irrespective of socio-economic status. The organization now provides mentorship, research support, and collective academic life to students from various continents with the aim of fostering a multicultural and inclusive community of research. NECHR's initiative still benefits the students by allowing them to make significant inputs in global intellectual discussions and innovations.
