Welcome to Northeastern Centre for High School Research

Connecting High Schoolers and Collegiate Students!

Advancing Research and Academic Excellence

NECHR provides high school students with structured research opportunities, no-cost mentorship, and a platform to engage in academic discourse at an advanced level.

What NECHR Offers

  • • One-on-one mentorship with current college students
  • • Opportunities for research publication
  • • Access to academic networks and conferences
  • • Support for structured research methodologies
  • • Guidance on university applications and scholarships

Why Research in High School?

  • • Develops critical thinking and analytical skills
  • • Strengthens college applications with substantial work
  • • Provides early exposure to academic research standards
  • • Builds a foundation for future academic and professional success
  • • Encourages independent inquiry and intellectual growth
